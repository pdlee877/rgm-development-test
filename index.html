Creating Extraordinary Smiles

Welcome to Straffon Orthodontics

At Straffon Orthodontics, each individual’s treatment plan is as unique as their fingerprint; each smile, a personal signature. From invisible options, to customized virtual treatment plans that eliminate the need for impressions with 3D scanning technology, Straffon Orthodontics is Denver’s Top Choice.

We offer options to fit all different age groups, lifestyles, and treatment needs. Dr. Straffon is actively involved with designing and implementing new advancements in orthodontic technology, so his patients always receive the most streamlined and efficient treatment possible.

Not only do we have an amazing staff that goes above and beyond to make your experience effortless, Dr. Straffon takes a more personal approach and values your input. You will find that he works closely with you throughout the course of treatment to ensure you receive the signature smile you have always dreamed of.
